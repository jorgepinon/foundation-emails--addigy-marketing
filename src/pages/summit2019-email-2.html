5 Reasons to Attend the Addigy Partner Summit

1. The speaker lineup is one you don’t want to miss

Our amazing speaker lineup continues to grow every week, so stay tuned, but right now we are very proud to present:

Keynote Speaker
Gene Munster

Gene Munster is Managing Partner at Loop Ventures and was Senior Research Analyst at Piper Jaffray as the most renowned Analyst globally of Apple. Gene continues to provide global analyst coverage of Apple (AAPL), Google (GOOG), Baidu.com (BIDU), and other technology, online media and search companies are included in his coverage. Gene’s awards have included Home Run Hitter from the Institutional Investor, Top Stock Picker from Forbes, and Best on the Street from the Wall Street Journal. (More from Street Insider).

Gene will provide insights into Apple’s roadmap and push into Business & Enterprise especially with deep comparison to Google and the overall ecosystem.

Rich Trouton
Rich Trouton has been doing Macintosh system and server administration for twenty years and has supported Macs in a number of different environments, including university, government, medical research, advertising and enterprise software development. His current position is at SAP, where he works with the rest of the Apple CoE team to support SAP’s Apple community.

Mario Cruz

Mario is a Director at Watsco Ventures’ whose mission is to invent, build, and deploy innovative technology and business models to accelerate Watsco, Inc.’s growth. Watsco Ventures supports entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs by providing access to opportunities, capital, space, mentoring and access to various business networks.

More speakers to be announced soon!

2. Exclusive Addigy insider information

In addition to the great speaker line-up, you’ll be the first to learn about our product roadmap, and receive useful, actionable knowledge regarding the latest innovations transforming the Addigy community. You’ll get first-hand demonstrations and discussions about some of the newest Addigy platform features and we’ll also share some of the exciting changes we have planned for 2019.

3. Connect with the Addigy community

One of the biggest benefits of attending the Partner Summit is meeting other Addigy admins, both new and experienced. This allows for engaging conversations to learn from each other and share experiences and best practices for managing Apple devices. Learning from other Addigy professionals and meeting Addigy employees face-to-face is a once-a-year opportunity you don’t want to miss.

The conference also helps us at Addigy create and solidify relationships with members of the Addigy community by hearing first-hand how they are doing, what their challenges are, and how Addigy can help solve those challenges. A knowledgeable and engaged Addigy community helps us all.

4. Direct Access to the Addigy Team

Get those burning Addigy questions answered on the spot directly from the Addigy team! Many members of our team will be there for one-on-ones to discuss any of your questions or concerns. You’ll have plenty of access to our Product, Customer Success, and Development teams as well as the company’s leadership, all in a casual and fun environment.

5. Have warm fun on land and sea - Miami, Florida in the winter time

While the rest of the country is blanketed in snow in March, Miami is typically 69 to 75 degrees with virtually no rain or humidity, making it the perfect winter playground for you to escape to. Pair that with the location of the Summit, the Hotel Intercontinental Miami, which is in the heart of the city and minutes from the beaches, you’ve got the perfect recipe for a winter getaway while learning about all things Addigy.
