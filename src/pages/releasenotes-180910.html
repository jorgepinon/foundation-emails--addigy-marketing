New Addigy Update

September 10, 2018

MDM Enhancements • Faster Loading Devices • UI Improvements

What’s New

We’re excited to share a late summer update with our community regarding the latest Addigy release available now. These are some of the highlights:

  • MDM Updates: Escrowing FileVault keys, Updating GoLive to with MDM configs, more control over Login Window settings, and deployments now every 30 minutes.
  • UI Updates: Site-wide input fields and buttons updated, improved policy views, upgraded ScreenConnect (ver. 6.7)
  • Many Bug Fixes: Live Terminal for subdomains, much faster devices table, and much more.

Addigy users can immediately begin seeing these new features as soon as they log in. Be sure to read our latest blog post.
