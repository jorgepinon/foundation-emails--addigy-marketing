Welcome to Addigy!

Addigy is the world’s leading Cloud RMM platform focused on Apple Device Management. We are excited to have you as a partner within the Addigy Community. Addigy provides a full RMM Suite with seamless integrations into many different frameworks, tools, and authentication providers.

Here are a few useful links to help you make the most of Addigy:

Creating Policies

Policies are complete configurations for your devices. Configure your policies and Addigy will apply and enforce those settings to any devices you add to that policy. Define your policies to match your organization: by department, client, or any subgroup.

Configure polices

Adding devices to Addigy

Enroll devices easily in a couple of different ways. Find them all on the Downloads page.

Via Direct install (and MDM)

Using either a custom URL a simple curl command, you can enroll a device into any policy you've created.

Select your install URL

Via DEP, Apple Business Manager

Setting up Addigy with your DEP is very easy and will install Addigy on any new devices during the intitial setup process. Those devices will check in as soon as they frist connect to the web and will appear in your Addigy account for you to manage.

Set up DEP on the Integrations page

Large, updated software catalog

Addigy includes a catalog of software that you can easily assign to a policy or deploy directly to any of your devices.

Integrations with support platforms

Addigy can automatically generate and close support tickets with FreshDesk, Zendesk, and ConnectWise services as needed.

Pricing

Addigy provides a monthly subscription-model pricing – you pay only for what you use and nothing more. Cancel any time.

Trial Period

After signing up, you have a 14-day trial period that includes all Addigy features, where you can discover all the value of Addigy in much shorter time than you ever expected. After your trial, simply enter your credit card information to continue using Addigy.

Other core features

Custom monitoring (with automatic remediation), reports, custom software deployment, direct secure shell access, and more.

Support Team

Addigy has the best support in the industry and it's completely free for all users.

Knowledge Base Articles: provide a wealth of FAQs, Training, and Troubleshooting information.

Training Videos: such as ‘Core Skills’, ‘Executive Seminar’, ‘Device Management’, and many more are available. Reach out to training@addigy.com to get a list of the current schedule.
