Welcome to Addigy!

Addigy is the world’s leading Cloud RMM platform focused on Apple Device Management. We are excited to have you as a partner within the Addigy Community. Addigy provides a full RMM Suite with seamless integrations into many different frameworks, tools, and authentication providers.

Here are a few useful links to help you make the most of Addigy:

Addigy Login

See and manage all your Apple devices from the web.

Create additional user accounts

Additional management accounts can be created in Addigy under Support > Users to give access to other team members.

Deploying the Addigy Agent

Enroll devices easily by remotely installing the Addigy Agent. There are few ways to do that. Find them all on the Downloads page.

Support Resources

Knowledge Base Articles: provide a wealth of FAQs, Training, and Troubleshooting information.

Training Videos: such as ‘Core Skills’, ‘Executive Seminar’, ‘Device Management’, and many more are available. Reach out to training@addigy.com to get a list of the current schedule.

Feature Requests and Bug Reporting: We encourage our clients to tell us what features would improve their Addigy workflows. You can submit, browse, and upvote features and bug reports within Addigy under Support > Issues.

Contact Support: Support can be reached from within Addigy under Support > Issues, emailing support@addigy.com, or by phone.
